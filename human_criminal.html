Defense 12; Health 16; Insanity d3-1; Corruption d3-1
Strength 12 (+0), Agility 12 (+2), Intellect 10 (+0), Will 9 (-1)
Speed 10; Professions Murderer
Trickery Once per round, the hire killer can make a challenge roll or an attack roll with 1 boon.
Backstab Once per round, when the hired killer attacks with a basic or swift weapon and makes the attack roll with at least 1 boon, the attack deals 1d6 extra damage.

attack options

Large Knife (melee) +2 (1d6 plus 2d6 if the hired killer had 1 boon from Trickery)

special attacks

Garrote The hired killer uses an action to grab (Strength or Agility attack roll against the target’s Agility) a Size 2 or less creature wielding the garrote with both hands. If the hired killer gets a success on the attack roll, the target takes 1d6 damage, becomes grabbed and cannot talk while grabbed this way. If the target attempts to escape, it makes its attack roll with 1 bane.

possessions

A drawing showing the face of the current contract, a pouch with 1d6 ss (depending of the difficulty of the job) and scoundrel’s gloves.

thief

diff 25

Size 1 human thief

Perception 13 (+3)
Defense 13; Health 24; Insanity d3; Corruption d3
Strength 10 (+0), Agility 13 (+3), Intellect 12 (+2), Will 10 (+0)
Speed 10; Professions Gambler and pickpocket
Trickery Once per round, the thief can make a challenge roll or an attack roll with 1 boon.

attack options

Dagger (melee or short range) +3 (1d3 plus 1d6 if the pickpocket had 1 boon from Trickery)

special attacks

Subterfuge The thief makes an Intellect attack roll against the Intellect of one creature within short range that can hear and understand what the thief says. On a success, the target becomes charmed for 1 round or until the thief attacks it. On a failure, the target becomes immune to the thief’s use of Subterfuge until the target completes a rest.

special actions

Escape Artist When the thief is grabbed, it can use a triggered action to escape. When rope bindings or manacles restrains the thief, it can use an action to attempt to escape the bonds. The thief must concentrate for 1 minute. At the end of this time, it makes an Agility challenge roll. On a success, it escapes the bonds. On a failure, it is bound tight and it remain bound until freed.
Pick PocketsThe thief uses an action or a triggered action on its turn, to take an object from a creature within its reach. The object taken must be something the thief can hold in one hand and be somewhere on the creature’s body but not held in its hands (or in a similar appendage). If the thief can’t see the object, because it reachs into a pouch or backpack, the item taken is determined randomly.

possessions

A deck of cards, a pair of dice and a pouch with 1d6 ss.

urchin

diff 1

Size ½ human

Perception 9 (-1)
Defense 12; Health 8; Insanity d3-1; Corruption 1d3-2
Strength 8 (-2), Agility 12 (+2), Intellect 9 (-1), Will 8 (-2)
Speed 10; Professions Urchin

attack options

Rock (short range) +2 (1)

spacial attacks

Pick Pockets The urchin makes an Agility attack roll against the Perception of one creature it can reach to take an object from it. The object must be something the urchin can hold in one hand and be somewhere on the creature’s body but not held in its hands. If the urchin can’t see the object, because it is into a pouch or backpack, it makes the attack roll with 1 bane. On a success, the urchin takes the object and the target is unaware that the urchin took it. On a failure, the urchin triggers a free attack from the target.
