"Mortui Vivos Docent"

physician

diff 10

Size 1 human rogue (leech)

Perception 13 (+3)
Defense 12 (soft leather); Health 16; Insanity d3; Corruption d3
Strength 10 (+0), Agility 10 (+0), Intellect 13 (+3), Will 10 (+0)
Speed 10; Professions Scholarship (medicine and surgery)
Expertise The physician makes rolls to stabilize creatures with 2 boons. In addition, if the physician tends to a resting character, the character heals extra damage equal to its healing rate and makes challenge rolls to resist the effects of disease and poison with 1 boon.
Plague Mask The physician makes Strength challenge rolls made to resist dangerous air or diseases which spreads through the air.
Trickery Once per round, the physician can make a challenge roll or an attack roll with 1 boon.

attack options

Rapier (melee) +0 (1d6 +1 plus 1d6 if the physician had 1 boon from Trickery)

special actions

Physician’s Cure The physician can use an action and expend a use from its healing kit to treat one target creature. The physician makes an Intellect challenge roll. On a success, the target heals 1 damage and the physician can remove one of the following afflictions from the target: fatigued, frightened, impaired, or poisoned. If the affliction has permanent duration, it cannot be removed using this talent.
Stabilize The physician can choose one incapacitated creature within your reach and then make an Intellect challenge roll, with 3 boons or 2 boons if the creature is dying. On a success, the target heals 1 damage.

possessions

An antitoxin potion, a healing kit (6 uses), a healing potion, a pouch with 1d6 ss and sawbones tools.
