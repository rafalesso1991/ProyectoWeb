apothecary

diff 1

Size 1 human

Perception 11 (+1)
Defense 10; Health 10; Insanity d3-2; Corruption d3-2
Strength 10 (+0), Agility 10 (+0), Intellect 11 (+1), Will 10 (+0)
Speed 10; Professions Apothecary or healer
Expertise The apothecary makes rolls to stabilize creatures with 2 boons. In addition, if the apothecary tends to a resting character, the character heals extra damage equal to its healing rate and makes challenge rolls to resist the effects of disease and poison with 1 boon.

special actions

Heal Wounds The apothecary can expend a use of its healer’s kit to tend to injuries of one creature within it's reach. The healer makes an Intellect challenge roll with 1 boon. On a success, the target heals 1 damage.
Stabilize The apothecary can choose one incapacitated creature within your reach and then make an Intellect challenge roll, with 2 boons or 1 boon if the creature is dying. On a success, the target heals 1 damage.

possessions

An alchemist kit, a healing kit (6 uses), a healing potion and a pouch with 2d6 cp.

special attacks

Subterfuge The merchant makes an Intellect attack roll against the Intellect of one creature within short range that can hear and understand what the merchant says. On a success, the target becomes charmed for 1 round or until the merchant attacks it. On a failure, the target becomes immune to the merchant’s use of Subterfuge until the target completes a rest.

A healing potion, a pouch with 2d6 ss, a rank 0 incantation and raw goods (such as textiles or grains) worth 1d6 ss.
